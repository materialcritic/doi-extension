Journal Watchlist

Subscribe to a journal from the "Watch This Journal" button on its Download-This-Issue page. Checked automatically every 6 hours in the background; when a new issue appears you'll get a notification you can click to open its download page.

Author Watchlist

Subscribe to an author from the "Watch This Author" button on their Download-All-Works page. Checked automatically every 6 hours in the background; when a new work appears you'll get a notification you can click to open their works page.
